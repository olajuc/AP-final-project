What is Pluto?

Pluto.markets is new player on the investing market. Our mission is to create a social, innovative investing app for the new generation of investors!

What are we building at Pluto?

As more people are investing, more people are also naturally talking about it. Investing has become social!! In particular, this is happening among groups of friends who use WhatsApp, Telegram, Messenger or similar applications to exchange view on the markets and share new interesting investment opportunities! At Pluto we are building the leading investing app for friend-groups enabling them to collaborate, compete and co-invest powered by fractional shares and zero-commission trading. Having the ability to share information with ease is one step towards increased financial literacy and can make the experience much mere engaging. Why are we different?

What makes it very unique is the social aspect of this application, where you can actually create an investing club with your closest friends, which means you can communicate and debate about your trades or the latest market changes. Pluto's mission is to, firstly, encourage more people to start investing since that's one of the most rapidly growing markets right now. Secondly, make sure when people invest, they are actually making more educated decisions by enriching it with financial data. See your friends' portfolios and get notified when they make a move that you might copy.

How we started?

The founders (Joakim & Oscar) have known each other since age 15 and have built another company together before. Joakim holds a MSc Degree in Economics and have worked as a trader at Danske Bank and Goldman Sachs while Oscar is BSc in Software Development and have spent most of his career building software for the pharma industry.
